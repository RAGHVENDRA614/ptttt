Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account on EcoLearn?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of our homepage. You'll need to provide your email address, create a password, and complete your profile information.

Is EcoLearn free to use?

Yes, EcoLearn offers a free tier with access to most of our educational content and games. We also offer a premium subscription with additional features and content for a small monthly fee.

How can I track my environmental impact?

Your profile includes an Impact Tracker that calculates the environmental benefits of your completed challenges and activities. You can view your carbon footprint reduction, water saved, and other metrics.

Can EcoLearn be used in classrooms?

Absolutely! We offer special educational plans for teachers and schools. Contact our education team at education@ecolearn.com to learn more about classroom features and discounts.
